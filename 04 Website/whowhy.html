Project Background

Irreversible climate change is occurring due to energy and natural resource usage trends. Facilities/energy managers need a tool to maximize availability while minimizing consumption based on vacancy patterns in buildings.

With the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic, buildings at Lawrence Berkeley National Labs saw occupancy levels drop by 90%, yet energy usage only dropped 20% due to buildings waking up and being put to sleep on pre-pandemic schedules.
